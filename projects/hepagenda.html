This project specifically aims to develop an application that allows the user to administrator book appointments more efficiently, view appointment schedule, fill in specific data about patients, attach files as well as view and then the patient's history. In this sense, aiming at a better monitoring and understanding of the situation of each one, in addition to facilitating the administration of physicians/system users who work in the clinic.

Development of an electronic diary for booking appointments for patients with hepatitis at the hepatitis clinic at Univerisade Federal de Minas Gerais.

Volunteer work as Scrum Master, Team Leader Backend and FullStack Developer.
